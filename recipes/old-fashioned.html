Old Fashioned Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube or 1/2 teaspoon of granulated sugar
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • 1 orange peel
  • ice

Preparation:

  1. In a rocks glass, muddle the sugar and bitters until dissolved.
  2. Add a large ice cube and pour in the whiskey.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with the orange peel.
