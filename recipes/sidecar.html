Sidecar Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz cognac or brandy
  • 1 oz Cointreau or triple sec
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • granulated sugar for rim (optional)
  • ice
  • lemon twist for garnish

Preparation:

  1. If using a sugar rim, wet the rim of a chilled cocktail glass and dip into sugar to coat.
  2. In a shaker with ice, combine the cognac or brandy, Cointreau or triple sec, and lemon juice.
  3. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.
