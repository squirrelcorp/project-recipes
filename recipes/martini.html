Martini Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 oz gin or vodka
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • ice
  • lemon twist or olive for garnish

Preparation:

  1. In a mixing glass with ice, stir together the gin or vodka and vermouth until chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a lemon twist or olive.
