Manhattan Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • ice
  • maraschino cherry for garnish

Preparation:

  1. In a mixing glass with ice, stir together the whiskey, vermouth, and bitters until chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a cherry.
